# Check Answer Workflow

The quiz now requires you to **check your answer before moving on**.

## How It Works

1. **Answer the Question**

   - **MCQ**: Click your choice
   - **Fill in blank**: Type your answer
   - **Multiple answers**: Select all that apply

2. **Check Your Answer**

   - Click the orange **"Check Answer"** button
   - You'll immediately see if you're ✓ Correct or ✗ Wrong

3. **Review Feedback**

   - ✓ **Correct** - Green highlight, encouragement message
   - ✗ **Wrong** - Red highlight, correct answer shown
   - For fill-in-the-blank, all acceptable answers are displayed

4. **Move to Next Question**
   - After checking, the **"Next"** button appears
   - Click to proceed to the next question
   - (On the last question, click "Submit Quiz" instead)

## Score Tracking

- Score updates in real-time at the top
- Only correct answers count toward your score
- Final score shown when you submit

## Key Features

- ✓ Can't skip questions without checking
- ✓ Can't change answer once checked
- ✓ Immediate feedback helps identify weak areas
- ✓ Real-time score tracking
